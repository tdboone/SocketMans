Inventory:

Welcome to Socket Mans! Use the left and right arrow keys to move and use the up arrow key to jump. Press the space bar to pick up a green block below you into your inventory, and press the space bar with a green block in your inventory to drop the block at your current location. More socket mans appear and disappear as other players join and leave.

(Careful with dropping blocks, as you can still get stuck in walls at this point.)
