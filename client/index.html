Inventory:

Welcome to Socket Mans! Use the left and right arrow keys to move and use the up arrow key to jump. Press the space bar to pick up a green block below you into your inventory, and press the space bar with a green block in your inventory to drop the block at your current location. You may also press against a wall to pick up a block to the left or to the right.

More socket mans appear and disappear as other players join and leave.

To chat, press enter, type a message, and then press enter again to send.
